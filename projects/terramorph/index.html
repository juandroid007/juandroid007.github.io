Terramorph

Terramorph is a project that I started about 6 months ago. It's a metroidvania game that I've been developing with the intention of having something completely own, creating my own tools and my own engine.

The game will take elements of games such as the classic Metroid and Cave Story (most of all of the latter).

In the current phase of the project there is not much to say, in addition to the demonstrations on my YouTube channel. With time I will be publishing demos so that they can be played.
